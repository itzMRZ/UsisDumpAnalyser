About Connect Dump Analyser

Purpose

This tool helps to explore courses across multiple semesters, with features for cross matching historic faculty placement data for TBA.

Faculty Prediction for TBA Courses

For courses with "TBA" faculty, the system predicts possible faculty assignments using:

  • Section History Match - Faculty who taught this section in previous semesters
  • Time Match - Faculty who taught this course at similar times
  • Room Match - Previously assigned rooms for this course [BETA]
